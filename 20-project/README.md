# 20-project — Your Archiving Plan / خطة الأرشفة الخاصة بك

**EN** — **This is where your assignment lives.** Your archiving plans, organized by what you intend to collect. Each sub-folder represents one collection stream (e.g., news articles, overseas archives, local interviews). The sub-folders below are examples — freely divide your plan by what you intend to collect.

## How to Use

1. In Week 6, use `@collection-plan` to draft your first plan here
2. One plan file per collection stream
3. Each plan should specify: scope, sources, methods, inclusion/exclusion criteria, timeline
4. Plans feed into `30-collected/` — the results of your plans accumulate there
