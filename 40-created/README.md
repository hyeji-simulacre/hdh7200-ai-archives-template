# 40-created — Knowledge You Produce / المعرفة التي تنتجها بنفسك

**EN** — Records **you create yourself**, not what you collect from outside. An archive is not only what you *find* — it is also what you *make* while building it. Your plans, observations, analyses, and conclusions are records too.

**AR** — السجلات التي **تنتجها بنفسك**، وليس ما تجمعه من الخارج. الأرشيف ليس فقط ما *تجده* — بل هو أيضًا ما *تصنعه* أثناء بنائه.

## Assignment Mapping

## You Are the Author / أنت المؤلف

Even if an AI agent helps you write these notes, **you are the author**. Review, edit, and rewrite anything the agent produces before saving it here. A created record that is pure AI output is not yours — and it does not belong in your archive.
